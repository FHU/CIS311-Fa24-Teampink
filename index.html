Chester County BBQ Festival

The Chester County BBQ Festival continues to be an annual tradition since 1978. The festival is held each year on the fourth weekend of September. It features BBQ, live entertainment, fried treats, children’s activities, and vendors. The festival is the Henderson/Chester County Chamber of Commerce’s largest fundraising event and is made possible by a volunteer committee. There is no admission fee. All events center around the Chester County Courthouse lawn in downtown Henderson. This year we will be hosting the 2nd Annual Tennessee State Hog Callin’ Championship!

BBQ Festival Hours

Friday: 10:00 am - 10:00 pm

Saturday: 9:00 am - 9:00 pm
